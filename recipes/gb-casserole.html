Green Bean Casserole

A Thanksgiving staple, a tried and true casserole of midwest church basements everywhere, and perfected by Paula Deen

Ingredients

  • 1/3 to 1/2 stick of Butter
  • 32oz carton of Chicken stock or broth
  • 2 cups sliced Fresh Mushrooms
  • 1 Medium Yellow/White Onion, diced
  • 2x 6oz Crispy Fried Onions
  • 3 cans of Cream of Mushroom soup
  • 2 cups Cheddar
  • 2 lbs Frozen green beans
  • House seasoning (recipe below)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet. Saute onions and mushrooms in the butter.
  3. Boil green beans in chicken broth for 10 minutes, or until al dente, and drain.
  4. Add green beans, mushroom soup, some cheddar, fried onions, and 1 tsp House seasoning and sauteed onions and mushrooms to large mixing bowl. Mix well.
  5. Pour into a greased lasagna or casserole dish. ~5 qt
  6. Bake for 20 minutes, then top the casserole with the cheddar cheese and bake for 10 minutes longer, or until the casserole is hot and cheese is melted and browned.

House seasoning

  • 4 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • Mix ingredients and store in an airtight container for up to 6 months.
